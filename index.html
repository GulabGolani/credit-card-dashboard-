🏆
Platinum Segment Leads Growth
Platinum cardholders contribute 38% of revenue while representing only 22% of the customer base — highest revenue-per-customer at $8,200 annually.
📍
Geographic Concentration Risk
Top 5 states — CA, TX, NY, FL, IL — account for 58% of total revenue. Expansion into Southeast markets shows +34% YoY growth potential.
⚡
Digital Spend Acceleration
E-commerce transactions grew 41.2% in FY2024. Contactless payments now represent 67% of in-store transactions, up from 49% in 2023.
🎯
Default Rate at 3-Year Low
Default rate improved to 2.14% from 2.45% in FY2023. Enhanced credit scoring model deployed in Q2 reduced high-risk approvals by 18%.
